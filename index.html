Details of generating realistic intensity variations in longitudinal images, implementation aspects and usage details of the software:

  1. Khanal, B., Ayache, N., & Pennec, X. (2016). Simulating Realistic Synthetic Longitudinal Brain MRIs with known Volume Changes. NeuroImage (under Review).
